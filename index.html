Hi, my name is Lilian Meier! I am currently a junior at Faith Lutheran High School in Las Vegas, NV. After college, I plan to pursue a career in real estate law. I have used Feel the Breeze Shampoo & Conditioner throughout my high school career, where I have performed in ballroom dancing, gymnastics, and ice skating. It takes out all the heavy gel and hairspray buildup while keeping my hair soft, healthy, and refreshingly clean!

My inspiration for founding Feel the Breeze started two years ago, when I spent my summer break volunteering in Sri Lanka. I joined an organization that gave a few of my peers and I the opportunity of a lifetime. Throughout my childhood, I often spent time giving back to others in my community, but my time in Sri Lanka was entirely different. It completely changed how I looked at the world, causing me to realize how much I take for granted every single day. The children I met didn't have parents, took cold showers every day and studied day and night. Also, the construction workers throughout the city wore no safety gear, much less shoes.

Despite all this, they took every opportunity to make me feel like family. Their kind, beautiful souls opened my eyes to the world beyond my life in Las Vegas. By starting Feel the Breeze, I now have the opportunity to share the love that the children in Sri Lanka shared with me. Feel the Breeze's Shampoo & Conditioner nourishes your hair with a healthy shine and the proceeds from this product go towards the children of Sri Lanka, so they may have a brighter future.
