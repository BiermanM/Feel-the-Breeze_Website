About Me

Hi, my name is Lilian Meier! I am currently a junior at Faith Lutheran High school in Las Vegas, NV. I aspire to be a real estate business lawyer. On my free time I love ballroom dancing. I starting competing since I was a child. I have used this product throughout my high school career where I have performed in ballroom, gymnastic, and ice skating. It takes out all the heavy gel and hairspray buildup without stripping the moisture while keeps my hair soft, healthy, and refreshingly clean.

How this came about was through not only giving people a chance to feel the breeze for their hair but also giving back to those in need. Two of my summers I spent two weeks volunteering in Sri Lanka. My organization gave me and a few others an opportunity of a life time at a young age. As a kid I spend my time giving back to those who are less fortunate. When I went to Sri Lanka my whole world flipped. I realized the life I live I take for granted. These kids don’t have parents, they take cold showers, they study day and night, the construction workers either work without shoes or wear flip flops without any safety gear on. The list goes on for how little they have but make the most out of it. After two weeks once it was time to go I did not want to leave. These people made me feel like family. Their kind beautiful souls opened my eyes more to the world. I wanted to do more for them in any way I can. Giving back in any way I can. My inspiration came from them. They are the root to all of this. Instead of making a big donation I started a company to give people more beneficial needs for their hair. With the money we make I also hope to put aside money to raise and give back to Sri Lanka.
